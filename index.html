Education


Flatiron School - Austin, TX

Full-Stack Web Development


I enrolled with the Flatiron School in the Summer of 2020 where I learned full-stack web development using Ruby on Rails and React.js.


Since graduation I've been working on better understanding of back-end technologies. While I love working on all parts of the software development lifecycle, fully understanding how and why things are operating "under the hood" is what intrigues me the most about software.


Universtiy of Houston - Houston, TX

Bachelor of Music Education


In 2015, I graduated Magna Cum Laude from the University of Houston where I studied Music Education under the tutelage of David Bertman, Marion West, John Benzer, Gabe Musella, and Terri Risinger.


During my time at U of H, I performed with the Symphonic Winds, the Carolina Crown Drum and Bugle Corps, and the Madison Scouts. I pledged Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia in the spring of 2012 and wrote articles for the Daily Cougar.
